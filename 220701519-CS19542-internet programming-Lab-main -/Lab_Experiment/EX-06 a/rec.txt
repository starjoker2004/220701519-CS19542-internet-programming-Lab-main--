Rajalakshmi Engineering College (REC) is a private engineering institution located in Thandalam, Sriperumbudur, near Chennai, Tamil Nadu. Established in 1997 by the Rajalakshmi Educational Trust, REC is affiliated with Anna University and approved by AICTE12.

Key Highlights:-

Accreditations: 
            REC is accredited by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) with an A++ Grade3.
Courses Offered: 
            The college offers a variety of programs, including 18 undergraduate, 9 postgraduate, and 8 doctoral programs3.
Facilities: 
            REC boasts state-of-the-art infrastructure, including modern laboratories, a library, hostels, and sports facilities4.
Placements: 
            The college has a strong placement record, with over 1640 placement offers for the batch of 20231.
Extracurricular Activities: 
            Students are encouraged to participate in various extracurricular activities such as sports, music, dance, and cultural festivals1.
